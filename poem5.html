Eternal Spring

In gardens where the flowers bloom, Despite the seasons' fickle loom, There lies a secret, evergreen, An eternal spring not often seen. Where hope takes root in fertile ground, And joy in every heart is found, Where love blooms fresh with morning dew, And dreams are born with colors new. So find within this place of yours, A spring that blooms through seasons' showers, Where every day can freshly start, And hope will always fill your heart.
