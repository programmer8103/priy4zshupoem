that easy?

is it really that easy? to stop talking to you, to stop asking do you need something? to stop wanting to know you? is it really that easy? then why does silence ache? why do unsent texts pile up like thoughts i can’t let go? why do i still wait for a reply that won’t come for a name that still feels like home? is it really that simple to unlove, to undo everything i once knew? because if it is then teach me because i am stuck in a goodbye that never said itself.
