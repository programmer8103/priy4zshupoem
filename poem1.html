The Morning Dew

The world awakens in gentle light, As dewdrops catch the morning's first sight. A silver crown on petals fair, A moment's beauty beyond compare. The air is crisp with freshness sweet, Where earth and sky briefly meet. In silence deep, the soul takes flight, On wings of dawn and morning light. So fleeting, yet so pure and true, This gift of morning, bright and new. A whispered promise, soft and clear, That each new day is drawing near.
